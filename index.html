Welcome to PSC IIT BHU

Welcome to UPSC Club IIT BHU, your one-stop destination for cracking the prestigious Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) exam and achieving your IAS, IPS, and other administrative dreams! Founded in [2024], our club is a vibrant community of dedicated students and experienced faculty, all driven by the common goal of empowering aspirants to excel in this challenging yet rewarding journey.

Since our inception, we've proudly witnessed [10] students scaling the UPSC Everest, securing top ranks like AIR [27, 49, 120, 252, 372, 673, 732, 825, 843, 850] and etching their names in the annals of Indian bureaucracy. We're not just numbers, though; we're a testament to the transformative power of unwavering dedication, expert guidance, and a supportive community.

Programs & Resources

Test Series & Mock Exams

Sharpen your skills and assess your readiness with our comprehensive test series, designed to mimic the real UPSC exam experience. Choose from full-length mocks covering all subjects, subject-specific tests for targeted practice, and daily quizzes for continuous improvement. Analyse your performance with detailed feedback reports and expert guidance.

Read more

Mentorship & Guidance

Our personalized mentorship program goes beyond academics. Get matched with a dedicated faculty mentor who will be your confidante, guide, and cheerleader throughout your UPSC journey. Enjoy one-on-one sessions for doubt clarification, strategic planning, and personalized feedback. Group discussions foster peer learning and camaraderie, while career counselling helps you navigate the diverse avenues of the Indian civil services.

Read more

Study Materials & Notes

Empower your preparation with our curated collection of study materials. Download comprehensive notes prepared by our experienced faculty, covering all UPSC syllabus topics in a concise and clear format. Access previous years' question papers with solutions to understand exam patterns and develop effective answering strategies. We also offer downloadable study guides, mind maps, and other valuable resources to optimize your learning.
